Mario Characters

Mario

Mario is the main character of the series and the hero of the Mushroom Kingdom. Known for his jumping abilities and iconic red hat, he is always ready to rescue Princess Peach from the clutches of Bowser.

Luigi

Luigi is Mario's younger brother and loyal sidekick. He is taller and more cautious than Mario, often helping him on his adventures. Luigi has his own unique set of abilities, including his higher jump.

Princess Peach

Princess Peach is the ruler of the Mushroom Kingdom. Often the damsel in distress, she has been kidnapped by Bowser numerous times. Despite this, she is a kind and brave leader who occasionally joins Mario on his quests.

Bowser

Bowser, also known as King Koopa, is the primary antagonist of the Mario series. He is a powerful and cunning villain who constantly schemes to capture Princess Peach and conquer the Mushroom Kingdom.

Toad

Toad is a loyal attendant of Princess Peach and a long-time friend of Mario and Luigi. Recognizable by his mushroom-like appearance, Toad is known for his cheerful personality and helpful nature.

Yoshi

Yoshi is a friendly dinosaur who assists Mario on many of his adventures. With his long tongue and ability to eat enemies, Yoshi is a valuable ally in the quest to save the Mushroom Kingdom.
